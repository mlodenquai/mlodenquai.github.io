ELOQUENT JF PRO REGULAR

Eloquent JF Pro is a modern serif which features highly contrasting thick and thin strokes that play a crucial role in contributing to it’s bold look. Featuring an inclined central axis, this typeface showcases numerous prominent features such as it’s soft, rounded terminals, as well as it’s signature circular tail.
